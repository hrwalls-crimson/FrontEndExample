Pancake Recipe!

9/10, 6/7/2010

Everyone loves pancakes, and they couldn't get any easier,
or tastier!



    For this recipe, you will need

  • 2 cup flour
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 whole egg

Mix it all together and pour a quarter cup of mix onto a well oiled pan.
Sear till golden brown and then flip (1-2 minutes). Repeat until finished.
